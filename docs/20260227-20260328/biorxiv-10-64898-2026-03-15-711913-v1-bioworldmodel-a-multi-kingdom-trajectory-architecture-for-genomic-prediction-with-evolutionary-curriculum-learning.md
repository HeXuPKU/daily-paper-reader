## 摘要
基因组预测模型通常在单一物种上进行训练，并忽略了时间动态，这些假设限制了它们的生物学适用范围。在本文中，我提出了 BioWorldModel，这是一种统一的架构，仅使用一组参数即可预测真菌、植物和动物的跨物种多性状表型分布。该模型引入了一个具有生物体条件注意力池化（organism-conditioned attention pooling）的可扩展基因型编码器、一个四通道生物记忆系统，以及一个具有对角方差参数化的高斯输出头。通过对跨越三个界的五种生物（酿酒酵母、拟南芥、黑腹果蝇、水稻、玉米；共 641 个性状）进行联合训练，该模型实现了 0.821 的生物体平均 R2（性状加权 R2 为 0.413），并显著优于在每个生物体上单独训练的 GBLUP、BayesB、Lasso 和随机森林等基准模型。这些结果表明，基因型到表型的映射在不同界之间遵循共同的原则，而单一模型可以利用这些原则。

## Abstract
Genomic prediction models are trained on single species and ignore temporal dynamics-- assumptions that limit their biological scope. Here I present BioWorldModel, a unified architecture that predicts multi-trait phenotypic distributions across fungi, plants, and animals with a single set of parameters. The model introduces a scalable genotype encoder with organism-conditioned attention pooling, a four-channel biological memory system, and a Gaussian output head with diagonal variance parameterization. Trained jointly on five organisms spanning three kingdoms (S. cerevisiae, A. thaliana, D. melanogaster, O. sativa, Z. mays; 641 traits total), the model achieves organism-averaged R2 = 0.821 (trait-weighted R2 = 0.413) and substantially outperforms GBLUP, BayesB, Lasso, and Random Forest baselines trained per organism. These results demonstrate that genotype-to-phenotype mapping follows shared principles across kingdoms that a single model can exploit.
