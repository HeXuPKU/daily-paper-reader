## 摘要
背景：多囊卵巢综合征（PCOS）是一种常见的内分泌疾病，也是导致女性不孕的主要原因，具有复杂的遗传、代谢和激素病因。长链非编码 RNA（lncRNA）已成为多种生物过程的重要调节因子，但其在 PCOS 中的作用仍有待深入探索。本研究采用结合表达数据、遗传关联和进化分析的综合方法，鉴定并表征了 PCOS 差异表达基因相关 lncRNA（PDEGAL）。方法：从我们之前的研究中获取了 33 个 PCOS 相关蛋白质编码基因，并对其附近及重叠的所有 lncRNA 进行了注释。利用 UCSC 基因组浏览器映射的注释和数据集（包括 NCBI RefSeq、GENCODE、GTEx、GWAS SNP 和保守性）以及 FANTOM5 基因表达帽分析（CAGE）启动子数据对这些候选基因进行分析，以评估其表达、调节潜力、遗传变异重叠和进化保守性。结果：共鉴定出 23 个 PDEGAL（其中 18 个与已知的 PCOS 相关蛋白质编码基因呈反义关系，5 个与其共享双向启动子）。17 个 PDEGAL 包含具有统计学显著疾病关联的 GWAS SNP，其中 9 个与 PCOS 相关性状有关。5 个 PDEGAL 在 PCOS 的 KGN 颗粒细胞模型中表现出表达。关键基因结构元件（KGSE）分析显示，大多数 PDEGAL 具有灵长类特异性。综合 GTEx 表达、GWAS SNP、FANTOM 启动子组和 KGSE 保守性这四个标准，HELLPAR 是唯一满足所有四个标准的 lncRNA，而另外五个（PGR-AS1、MTOR-AS1、ENSG00000265179、ENSG00000256218 和 LOC105377276）满足其中三个标准。结论：我们通过遗传、表达和进化证据的汇聚，系统地鉴定了候选 PCOS 调节性 lncRNA。这些结果为功能验证提供了一个框架，并强调了 lncRNA 作为 PCOS 潜在生物标志物和治疗靶点的价值，它们通过调节其附近和重叠的蛋白质编码基因发挥作用。

## Abstract
Background: Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a prevalent endocrine disorder and a leading cause of female infertility, with complex genetic, metabolic, and hormonal etiologies. Long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) have emerged as important regulators of diverse biological processes, yet their roles in PCOS remain underexplored. Here, we identified and characterized PCOS differentially expressed gene-associated lncRNAs (PDEGAL) with an integrative approach combining expression data, genetic association, and evolutionary analysis. Methods: Thirty-three PCOS-associated protein-coding genes were obtained from our prior study, and all their nearby and overlapping lncRNAs were annotated. These candidates were analyzed using UCSC Genome Browser-mapped annotations and datasets, including NCBI RefSeq, GENCODE, GTEx, GWAS SNPs, and conservation, as well as the FANTOM5 cap analysis of gene expression (CAGE) promoter data, to assess their expression, regulatory potential, genetic variant overlaps, and evolutionary conservation. Results: Twenty-three PDEGALs (18 antisense to, and 5 sharing bidirectional promoters with, known PCOS-associated protein-coding genes) were identified. 17 PDEGALs contained GWAS SNPs with statistically significant disease associations, 9 of which were associated with PCOS-related traits. 5 PDEGALs demonstrated expression in the KGN granulosa cell model of PCOS. Key gene structure element (KGSE) analysis revealed that most PDEGALs are primate-specific. Integrating four criteria-GTEx expression, GWAS SNPs, FANTOM promoterome, and KGSE conservation-highlighted HELLPAR as the only lncRNA fulfilling all four, while five others-PGR-AS1, MTOR-AS1, ENSG00000265179, ENSG00000256218, and LOC105377276-fulfilled three of the four criteria. Conclusions: We have systematically identified candidate PCOS regulatory lncRNAs with convergent genetic, expression, and evolutionary evidence. These results provide a framework for functional validation and highlight lncRNAs as potential biomarkers and therapeutic targets in PCOS that function by regulating their nearby and overlapping protein-coding genes.
