## 摘要
预训练语言模型有望利用大规模文本语料库，为单细胞数据分析增添额外的信息层。然而，如何实现与原始定量单细胞数据的最佳对齐仍不明确。为了解决这一问题，我们针对当前的实验设置，从 scRNA-seq 数据和生物医学文献中构建了基于文本的训练数据集。随后，我们在两种信息源上联合训练语言模型，以学习一种通用的、富含文献信息的表示。我们在功能性、疾病关联和时间轨迹方面的示例展示了知识增强嵌入作为一种可泛化且可解释的策略，在丰富单细胞分析流程方面的潜力。

## Abstract
Pre-trained language models promise to enrich analyses of single-cell data with additional layers of information leveraging large text corpora. Yet, it is still unclear how to achieve optimal alignment with the primary quantitative single-cell data. To address this, we construct text-based training datasets from both scRNA-seq data and biomedical literature targeted to the experimental setting at hand. We then jointly train language models on both information sources to learn a common, literature-enriched representation. Our examples on functionality, disease associations, and temporal trajectories show the potential of knowledge-augmented embeddings as a generalizable and interpretable strategy for enriching single-cell analysis pipelines.
