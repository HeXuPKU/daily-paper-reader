## 摘要
木薯是热带地区的主要粮食作物，提高其根的营养品质，特别是类胡萝卜素和干物质含量（DMC），仍然是核心育种目标。为了阐明这些性状的遗传基础，定位与之相关的基因组区域，我们分析了来自巴西农业研究公司（Embrapa）育种项目的3,043个木薯克隆，这些克隆在2011年至2022年期间在巴西进行的188个多环境试验中进行了表型鉴定。所有克隆均通过基因分型测序（27,045个单核苷酸多态性 - SNPs）和多样性阵列技术 - DArTseq（25,923个SNPs）进行了基因分型。使用两阶段混合模型估计性状值以获得去回归BLUPs（最佳线性无偏预测），并采用混合线性模型（MLM）和多基因座混合模型（MLMM）进行全基因组关联分析。在Bonferroni校正后，我们检测到六个与类胡萝卜素含量和DMC一致相关的显著SNPs。这些SNPs定位到六个候选基因，这些基因参与与根生理相关的通路，包括脱落酸ABA相关信号、影响类胡萝卜素转化的水解酶活性、质体内脂肪酸生物合成、细胞壁重塑和糖酵解能量代谢。这些基因座共同解释了类胡萝卜素表型方差的75.56%和DMC的76.23%，单个SNP效应范围为PVE（解释方差比例）的约17%至约42%。广义遗传力为类胡萝卜素H² = 0.78，DMC的H² = 0.34，证实了显著的遗传控制和分子育种的适用性。单倍型分析揭示了四个类胡萝卜素的优势单倍型和一个DMC的关键单倍型，每个单倍型与其他等位基因组合相比均表现出显著更高的性状值。这些单倍型代表了标记辅助选择和基因组选择的有前景的目标，可直接应用于加速精英育种群体的遗传增益。这些结果为旨在开发生物强化和高根品质品种的育种项目提供了可操作的基因组资源，并为未来的多组学和功能验证研究奠定了基础。

## Abstract
Cassava is a major staple crop in tropical regions, and improving its root nutritional quality, particularly carotenoid and dry matter content (DMC), remains a central breeding goal. To elucidate the genetic basis of these traits by locating genomic regions associated with them, we analyzed 3,043 cassava clones from the Brazilian Agricultural Research Corporation (Embrapa) breeding program, phenotyped across 188 multi-environment trials conducted from 2011 to 2022 in Brazil. All clones were genotyped using Genotyping-by-Sequencing (27,045 Single Nucleotide Polymorphism - SNPs) and Diversity Arrays Technology - DArTseq (25,923 SNPs). Trait values were estimated using a two-stage mixed model to obtain deregressed BLUPs (Best Linear Unbiased Predictions), and genome-wide association analyses were performed using both the Mixed Linear Model (MLM) and Multi-Locus Mixed Model (MLMM). We detected six significant SNPs consistently associated with carotenoid content and DMC after Bonferroni correction. These SNPs mapped to six candidate genes involved in pathways relevant to root physiology, including Abscisic Acid ABA-related signaling, hydrolase activity affecting carotenoid conversion, fatty-acid biosynthesis within plastids, cell-wall remodeling, and glycolytic energy metabolism. The loci jointly explained 75.56 % of the phenotypic variance for carotenoids and 76.23 % for DMC, with individual SNP effects ranging from [~]17 % to [~]42 % PVE (Proportion of Variance Explained). Broad-sense heritability was H2 = 0.78 for carotenoids and H{superscript 2} = 0.34 for DMC, confirming substantial genetic control and suitability for molecular breeding. Haplotype analyses revealed four superior haplotypes for carotenoids and one key haplotype for DMC, each showing significantly higher trait values compared with other allelic combinations. These haplotypes represent promising targets for marker-assisted selection and genomic selection, with direct applicability for accelerating genetic gain in elite breeding populations. The results provide actionable genomic resources for breeding programs aiming to develop biofortified and high-root quality cultivars and establish a foundation for future multi-omics and functional validation studies.
