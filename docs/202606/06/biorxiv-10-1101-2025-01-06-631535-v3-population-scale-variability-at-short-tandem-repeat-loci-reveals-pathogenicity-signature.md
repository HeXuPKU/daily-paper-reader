## 摘要
短串联重复（STR）不稳定是疾病关联的主要原因，然而，使用短读长测序难以解析其完整的长度分布和序列内容。通过分析来自“我们所有人”研究计划（All of Us Research Program）2645名参与者的长读长测序基因组，我们建立了一个涵盖440万个位点目录、230亿个等位基因的全面STR资源。我们发现已知与疾病相关的STR位点相对于全基因组分布表现出显著升高的等位基因变异性，这种差异在基于不间断纯重复基序长度时最为明显（比值比：244，p=1.62e-29）。我们定义了纯长度变异性指数（PLVI）作为这种不稳定性的度量，该指数在不同祖先中稳健，并在来自1000基因组计划的500个样本中得到验证（比值比：84，p=2.72e-28）。举例来说，我们利用该指标识别出55个不稳定的编码区CAG和5'-UTR CGG STR位点，其中60%尚未与疾病相关联。通过将PLVI与单基因组长度异常值分析相结合，我们记录了FAM193B和EP400的潜在致病性重复扩增。这些数据为利用长读长测序队列在罕见病和常见病中发现STR建立了群体规模的框架。

## Abstract
Short tandem repeat (STR) instability is a major cause of disease association, yet their full length-distributions and sequence content have been difficult to resolve with short-read sequencing. By analyzing long-read sequencing genomes from 2,645 participants of the All of Us Research Program, we establish a comprehensive STR resource of 23 billion alleles covering a catalog of 4.4 million loci. We find that known disease-associated STR loci exhibit markedly elevated allelic variability relative to genome-wide distributions, which is most pronounced when based on the length of uninterrupted, pure repeat motifs (Odds Ratio: 244, p=1.62e-29). We establish the Pure Length Variability Index (PLVI) as a measure of this instability, which is robust across ancestries and replicated in 500 samples from the 1000 Genomes Project (Odds Ratio: 84, p=2.72e-28). To illustrate, we identify 55 unstable coding CAG and 5'-UTR CGG STR loci with this metric, 60% of which have not been associated with disease. From this set, we document potential pathogenic repeat expansions for FAM193B and EP400 by integrating PLVI with single-genome length outlier analysis. These data establish a population-scale framework for STR discovery in rare and common diseases using long-read sequencing cohorts.
